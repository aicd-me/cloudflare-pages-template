Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the AI Life Operating System.

Is my data truly private?

Yes. Your data never leaves your personal secure enclave. The AI runs locally when possible and uses federated learning techniques to protect your privacy.

How is this different from Siri or Alexa?

Siri and Alexa are voice interfaces that execute simple commands. AI Life OS is a cognitive partner that understands context, learns your patterns, works across all platforms, and can perform complex tasks autonomously.

What is the business model if you're not selling my data?

We operate on a subscription model. You pay for the service directly, which means we work for you, not advertisers. Your data remains yours alone.

Can I transfer my AI OS if I switch devices?

Absolutely. Your AI Life OS is platform-agnostic and follows you wherever you go—from Apple to Android, Windows to Mac, and across any new devices you acquire.

What happens if I want to stop using the service?

You can export all your data and delete your account at any time. We believe in giving users complete control and ownership.
